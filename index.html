Privacy Policy for InstaScan

Last updated: 2026

InstaScan ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application handles information when you use the app.

Overview

InstaScan is a document scanning application that allows users to scan documents using their device camera and save them as images or PDF files.

All document processing happens directly on your device. We do not collect, upload, or store scanned documents on any server.

Camera Access

The app requires access to your device camera to allow document scanning. The camera is used only for capturing document images within the app.

Local Storage

All scanned documents are stored locally on your device. The app does not upload documents to any external servers unless you explicitly choose to share them using your device’s built-in sharing features.

Data Collection

InstaScan does not collect personal information, scanned documents, or files created using the app.

Third-Party Services

The app may use limited third-party tools for crash reporting or analytics to improve app stability. These tools may collect anonymous technical information such as device type or app version.

Data Security

Because scanned documents remain on your device, you retain full control of your files. We recommend protecting your device with a passcode or biometric security.

Children's Privacy

InstaScan does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
